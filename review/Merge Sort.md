#### Merge Sort
- Divide and conquer, recursively
- 先从中间分段, merge sort 左边 (dfs), merge sort 右边
- 最后merge起来
- merge的时候因为是做int[], 所以没办法必须要O(n) space
- Time O(nlogn), Space O(n)
